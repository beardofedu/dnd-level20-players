# ⚔️ D&D 5e Player Cheat Sheet

A quick reference for common mechanics during play, especially in combat.

---

## 🗓️ Your Turn Structure

Each round of combat, you can use the following **in any order**:

| Resource | Description |
|---|---|
| **Movement** | Move up to your speed (default 30 ft). You can split this movement before and after your action. |
| **Action** | Your main activity each turn (attack, cast a spell, dash, etc.). |
| **Bonus Action** | Only if a feature, spell, or item specifically grants it. |
| **Reaction** | Once per round, triggered by a specific event (Opportunity Attack, Shield spell, etc.). Resets at the start of your turn. |
| **Free Interaction** | Interact with one object for free (draw a weapon, open a door). A second object interaction costs your action. |

---

## ⚡ Common Actions

| Action | What It Does |
|---|---|
| **Attack** | Make one or more weapon or unarmed attacks. |
| **Cast a Spell** | Cast a spell with a casting time of 1 action. |
| **Dash** | Gain extra movement equal to your speed for this turn. |
| **Disengage** | Your movement doesn't provoke opportunity attacks for the rest of this turn. |
| **Dodge** | Until the start of your next turn, attack rolls against you have disadvantage, and you have advantage on Dex saves. Requires you can see the attacker. |
| **Help** | Aid an ally's ability check or attack roll (they gain advantage on their next relevant roll vs. the creature you helped with). |
| **Hide** | Make a Dexterity (Stealth) check. Must have cover or be heavily obscured. |
| **Ready** | Prepare an action to trigger on a specific condition before your next turn. Holding a spell requires concentration. |
| **Search** | Make a Wisdom (Perception) or Intelligence (Investigation) check to find something. |
| **Use an Object** | Interact with a second object, or activate a magic item that requires your action. |
| **Grapple** | Attack action variant — use one attack to grab: contested Athletics vs. Athletics or Acrobatics. Grappled speed = 0. |
| **Shove** | Attack action variant — use one attack to push 5 ft or knock prone: contested Athletics vs. Athletics or Acrobatics. |

## 🎲 Attack Rolls

```
d20 + Ability Modifier + Proficiency Bonus (if proficient) vs. Target's AC
```

- **Hit** → Roll damage.
- **Miss** → No effect.
- **Critical Hit (natural 20)** → Roll all damage dice **twice**, then add modifiers once.
- **Critical Miss (natural 1)** → Automatic miss regardless of bonuses.

### Advantage & Disadvantage
- **Advantage** → Roll 2d20, take the **higher**.
- **Disadvantage** → Roll 2d20, take the **lower**.
- They cancel each other out (one of each = roll normally, regardless of how many sources).

## 💀 Death Saving Throws

When you drop to 0 HP you fall unconscious and start making **Death Saving Throws** at the start of each of your turns.

- Roll a **d20** (no modifiers unless a feature says otherwise).
- **10 or higher** → Success. Three successes = **stabilized** (0 HP, unconscious, no longer dying).
- **9 or lower** → Failure. Three failures = **dead**.
- **Natural 20** → Regain 1 HP and regain consciousness.
- **Natural 1** → Counts as **two** failures.
- Taking damage while at 0 HP = one failure (critical hit = two failures).
- Successes and failures reset when you regain any HP.

## 🩺 Healing & Hit Points

| Method | Notes |
|---|---|
| **Short Rest (≥1 hour)** | Spend Hit Dice (d[class size] + CON mod) to recover HP. |
| **Long Rest (≥8 hours)** | Regain all HP and recover half your max Hit Dice (minimum 1). Resets most spell slots & class features. |
| **Stabilize** | DC 10 Wisdom (Medicine) check on an unconscious creature stops death saves without restoring HP. |
| **Healing Potion** | Action to drink (or Bonus Action for yourself — this is a common house rule; check with your DM): 2d4+2 HP. Greater = 4d4+4. Superior = 8d4+8. Supreme = 10d4+20. |

## 🔮 Spellcasting Quick Reference

| Term | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Spell Attack Roll** | d20 + Spellcasting Ability Mod + Proficiency Bonus vs. AC |
| **Saving Throw DC** | 8 + Proficiency Bonus + Spellcasting Ability Modifier |
| **Concentration** | Maintaining certain spells. Taking damage = Constitution saving throw (DC = 10 or half damage, whichever is higher). Fail = spell ends. Only one concentration spell at a time. |
| **Spell Slots** | Expended to cast spells. Recovered on a Long Rest (Warlocks: Short Rest). |
| **Cantrips** | Level 0 spells. Never expend slots. Scale with character level (not class level). |
| **Upcasting** | Spending a higher-level slot on a lower-level spell for bonus effects (if the spell supports it). |
| **Ritual Casting** | Cast certain spells without expending a slot by taking 10 extra minutes. Must have Ritual Caster feature or class ability. |
| **Reaction Spells** | Casting time is 1 Reaction. Examples: *Shield*, *Counterspell*, *Hellish Rebuke*. |

## 🧠 Ability Checks & Saving Throws

```
d20 + Ability Modifier [+ Proficiency Bonus if proficient] vs. Difficulty Class (DC)
```

### Common DCs
| Difficulty | DC |
|---|---|
| Very Easy | 5 |
| Easy | 10 |
| Medium | 15 |
| Hard | 20 |
| Very Hard | 25 |
| Nearly Impossible | 30 |

### Saving Throw Proficiencies
Each class grants proficiency in **two saving throws**. Check your class entry.

## 🦠 Common Conditions

| Condition | Key Effect |
|---|---|
| **Blinded** | Can't see. Attack rolls against you have advantage; your attacks have disadvantage. |
| **Charmed** | Can't attack the charmer. Charmer has advantage on social checks against you. |
| **Deafened** | Can't hear. Automatic fail on checks requiring hearing. |
| **Exhaustion** | 6 levels. Each level stacks penalties (disadvantage → speed halved → checks/saves disadvantage → speed 0 → death). |
| **Frightened** | Disadvantage on ability checks and attacks while source is in sight. Can't move closer to source. |
| **Grappled** | Speed = 0. Ends if grappler is incapacitated or you move out of reach. |
| **Incapacitated** | Can't take Actions or Reactions. |
| **Invisible** | Can't be seen. Attacks against you have disadvantage; your attacks have advantage. |
| **Paralyzed** | Incapacitated, can't move/speak. Auto-fail Str/Dex saves. Attacks have advantage; hits within 5 ft are critical hits. |
| **Petrified** | Incapacitated, transformed to stone. Auto-fail Str/Dex saves. Resistance to all damage; immune to poison & disease. |
| **Poisoned** | Disadvantage on attack rolls and ability checks. |
| **Prone** | Movement cost: stand up = half speed. Attacks against you: melee advantage, ranged disadvantage. Your attacks: disadvantage. |
| **Restrained** | Speed = 0. Attacks against you have advantage; your attacks have disadvantage. Disadvantage on Dex saves. |
| **Stunned** | Incapacitated, can't move. Auto-fail Str/Dex saves. Attacks against you have advantage. |
| **Unconscious** | Incapacitated, drop prone. Auto-fail Str/Dex saves. Attacks have advantage; hits within 5 ft are critical hits. |

## 🗺️ Movement & Positioning

| Terrain / Action | Cost |
|---|---|
| Normal movement | 1 ft per 1 ft of speed |
| Difficult terrain | 2 ft per 1 ft of speed |
| Crawling | 2 ft per 1 ft of speed |
| Stand from prone | Half your speed |
| Climb / Swim | 2 ft per 1 ft (without a climb/swim speed) |
| Long Jump (running) | = your Strength score in feet |
| Long Jump (standing) | = half your Strength score |
| High Jump (running) | = 3 + STR modifier (in feet) |

### Cover
| Cover Type | Bonus |
|---|---|
| Half Cover (low wall, creature) | +2 to AC and Dex saves |
| Three-Quarters Cover (portcullis, thick tree) | +5 to AC and Dex saves |
| Full Cover | Can't be directly targeted |

## 🏹 Range & Area of Effect

- **Ranged attacks into melee**: Disadvantage if a hostile creature is within 5 ft and not incapacitated.
- **Long range**: Disadvantage on ranged attack rolls if target is beyond normal range (but within long range).

### Common AoE Shapes
| Shape | Description |
|---|---|
| **Cone** | Origin point, widens to listed width at the far end |
| **Cube / Square** | All creatures in the listed side-length square |
| **Cylinder** | Circular area, height specified |
| **Line** | Straight path of listed length and width |
| **Sphere** | Radius from a center point |

---

## 🎯 Opportunity Attacks

- Triggered when a hostile creature **you can see** moves **out of your reach**.
- Cost: your **Reaction**.
- Use your **Attack action** (one attack only, even if you have Extra Attack).
- **Does NOT trigger** if the creature uses Disengage or if teleported/moved by an outside force.

---

## ✨ Inspiration

- Awarded by the DM for good roleplay, creative thinking, or embodying your character.
- You can hold only **1 Inspiration** at a time.
- Spend it to gain **Advantage** on any d20 roll (attack, ability check, or saving throw).
- You may give your Inspiration to another player.
